Disability

No one chooses to become disabled. No one chooses to get hurt in an accident or become seriously sick. Long-term disability policies are designed to protect you when you can no longer work because of an injury or illness.

Termination

Even if the insurance company has initially approved your claim, they will too often cut off your benefits prematurely. You have not yet recovered from your illness or injury and are still disabled. The insurance company tells you that you can work in some other type of job, such as a parking lot attendant or ticket clerk.

Denial

Perhaps you are suffering from an illness like Fibromyalgia that causes complete pain throughout your body. Perhaps you are experiencing severe depression and anxiety. Some days you are so fatigued that you cannot even get out of bed.
